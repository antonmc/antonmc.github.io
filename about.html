I'm Anton, an Irish/Canadian software designer and developer, with a passion for building mobile solutions in iOS or HTML.

hickory.ca is my online home, to show some projects, and write down a few thoughts.

I'm especially interested in mining Twitter for insight, exploring data journalism, and pursuing the use of software for non profit causes.

I have a wonderful job as a developer advocate in IBM's cloud unit, and really like working with clients in diverse industries.

As part of my work I sometimes travel to speak at conferences, meetups or software labs - lately about personal and location awareness.

In my spare time I like to read, write, draw, run and play guitar.
