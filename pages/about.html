About

hickory.ca is the online home of Anton McConville, an Irish/Canadian web designer and developer, living and working in Ottawa, Canada.

I've been a developer, designer, manager, advisor. I've lived and worked in three countries, eleven different labs.

My work

My work with web software began more than a decade ago. Mostly it has been spent developing vector based software, and applications around table based data.

My most recent work has given me an opportunity to think more about visual design, user experience and user engagement.

My work with iOS began two years ago, and I love working in that medium.

I'm happy in my work :)
